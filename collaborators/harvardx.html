HarvardX first deployed IIIF resources and used Mirador as a stand alone application in a series of courses titled The Book: Histories Across Time and Space, where it showcased Harvard’s large digital collection. Since The Book, Mirador has been embedded into HarvardX’s Annotation Tool, and is used in other courses such as Visualizing Japan and ChinaX. Mirador allows faculty and learners to engage in focused discussions through the use of annotations to markup IIIF resources. HarvardX continues to explore new and interesting ways to enhance the experience of learners through the features of Mirador and IIIF.
